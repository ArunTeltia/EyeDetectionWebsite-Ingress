About

A simple web application that tracks the eyeball movement and guesses the alphabets to construct words.

How to play

The keyboard visible on your screen is divided into 2 parts - left and right. The app requires your camera access to track your eye movements. If you look right, the right part of keyboard is selected and it also gets divided into 2 subsequent parts and the similarly if you look left, the left part of keyboard is selected and it gets divided into 2 subsequent parts. The process keeps on running until we find a single word. Repeating this process multiple times will guess a word. Watch the demo to learn more.
